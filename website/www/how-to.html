How to play

Sign up and start drawing!

Before you can start drawing, you must frist create an accout, Sign up here. Fill ina ll the necessary information including first and last name, username, profile picutre, email, and password. Once you are signed up you are almost ready to start drawing. Drawesome allows users to connect with the people around you and collaborate on drawings. To start drawing you may either join an existing board and join others, or you can create a new board and allow others to join you!

Users can work on drawings for an allotted amount of time. Once drawings are finished, they are no longer allowed to be edited and are featured on the showcase page for everyone to see!
